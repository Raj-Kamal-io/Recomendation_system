Content-Based (TF-IDF)

Matches catalog titles and categories using TF-IDF text features. Highly effective for recommending content-similar items and bypasses cold-start issues for new items.

SVD Matrix Factorization

Decomposes sparse rating matrices into latent factors. Learns implicit ratings for items a user has not seen yet. Excels in absolute rating calibration but suffers from cold starts.

LightGCN GNN

Propagates embeddings across the user-item interaction graph. Multi-hop connections capture collaborative signals beyond sparse single ratings, yielding a 100%+ improvement in Precision/Recall.
